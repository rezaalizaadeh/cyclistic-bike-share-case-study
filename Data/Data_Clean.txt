Clean Dataset Description

This folder refers to the cleaned datasets generated from the raw
Cyclistic data covering November 2024 to October 2025.

The cleaned CSV files were produced using:
Scripts/load_and_clean.R

Cleaned files not uploaded (due to size ~800 MB total):
- all_trips_cleaned.csv
- all_trips_final.csv

Key Cleaning Steps:
- Combined 12 monthly CSVs (Nov 2024 → Oct 2025)
- Removed:
    • duplicate entries
    • negative or zero ride lengths
    • missing station names
    • missing user type (member/casual)
- Converted timestamps to POSIXct
- Engineered fields:
    • ride_length (minutes)
    • day_of_week
    • month
    • hour_of_day
    • weekend/weekday indicator
    • ride_type (member vs casual)

These cleaned datasets feed:
- Summary tables (in /Summaries)
- Visualizations (in /Visuals)
- The final report in /Final_report
